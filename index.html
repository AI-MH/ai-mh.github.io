Our Mission

AIMH (AI for Mental Health) is a research community dedicated to utilizing AI to advance mental health. Our work spans natural language processing and extends into broader AI applications, with a focus on developing innovative solutions for mental health challenges. Through rigorous research and open collaboration, we aim to bridge the gap between technology and clinical practice, ultimately improving outcomes for individuals worldwide. Join us on our journey to revolutionize mental health, connect with us on Discord and explore our latest findings.

Our Works

PsyDefConv

We introduce PsyDefConv (Psychological Defense Mechanisms in Supportive Conversations), a dialogue corpus with help seeker utterances labeled for defense level, and DMRS Co-Pilot, a four-stage pipeline that provides evidence-based pre-annotations.
