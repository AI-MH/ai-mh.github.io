Our Mission

AIMH is a research community dedicated to utilizing AI to advance mental healthcare. AIMH focuses on NLP techniques and beyond. Our ongoing research aims to enhance mental healthcare, and we're committed to sharing our findings through publications and presentations. Join in our Discord server for further information and collaborations as we strive to revolutionize mental healthcare with cutting-edge AI technology.
